7.5 跟踪记录表列表查询(根据日期recordDate排序降序)
http://localhost:8080/YXcrm/aaQuePageRecord?qqiu=pageByYXstuUid

请求方法:POST

请求头:
Content-Type: application/json;charset=UTF-8

请求主体:

{

"yxstuUuid":"u001",
"currentPage":"1",
"maxResult":"1"

}


响应：
status:200 OK
{
"message":"数据库总共页数：38",
